Calendly

Google Gemini

Leverage Google's AI for multilingual understanding, image recognition, and complex reasoning. Your chatbot handles nuanced questions, processes images customers send, and responds in their language — naturally.

Anthropic (Claude)

Trello

Asana

QuickBooks

Xero

For businesses that run on Xero, automate invoice creation, payment reconciliation, and expense categorization. Your chatbot handles the transaction, Xero handles the accounting — zero manual work.

Wise (TransferWise)

Zendesk

When your chatbot can't resolve it, Zendesk catches it. Auto-create support tickets with full conversation history, route to the right agent, and track resolution times — seamless escalation, zero context loss.

Freshdesk

Intercom

Google Drive

Dropbox
