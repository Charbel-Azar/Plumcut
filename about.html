Our Story

Three years ago, we started with a simple mission: help Lebanese businesses establish their digital presence. What began as designing informative websites and portfolios quickly evolved as we witnessed a transformation happening across the market.

When e-commerce exploded in Lebanon, we were ready. We built, launched, and scaled over 30 online stores , from fashion boutiques to electronics retailers, from artisanal food brands to service providers going digital for the first time. We didn't just hand over websites; we stayed. We watched. We learned.

And that's when we saw it.

Store owners weren't struggling to get sales , they were overwhelmed by everything that came after: customer inquiries piling up, leads going cold because no one replied in time, loyal customers slipping through the cracks because there was no system to track them, missed appointments and mixed-up orders.

That's how plumcut was born.

Today, we design intelligent chatbots and automation systems that don't just answer questions , they capture leads, qualify prospects, book appointments, process orders, handle complaints, and never take a day off. They speak your brand's voice. They remember your customers. They integrate with the tools you already use.

We're not here to replace the human touch. We're here to free you up so you can focus on what actually needs it.
